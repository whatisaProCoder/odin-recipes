Chicken Pizza

Description

Chicken Pizza is a deliciously savory dish that combines tender, flavorful chicken with a crisp pizza crust, topped with a variety of cheeses and vibrant toppings. The recipe begins by preparing or purchasing a pizza dough, which is rolled out and topped with a rich tomato or barbecue sauce as the base. The chicken is seasoned with Italian herbs, garlic, salt, and pepper, then either grilled or pan-cooked until tender and juicy. Once cooked, the chicken pieces are evenly spread across the sauce-covered crust.

Additional toppings such as sliced bell peppers, onions, olives, and sometimes mushrooms are layered on top, adding a mix of textures and flavors. The pizza is generously topped with shredded mozzarella or a blend of cheeses, then baked in a hot oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Garnished with fresh basil or parsley, chicken pizza is a delightful dish perfect for gatherings or a cozy night in, enjoyed best with a sprinkle of chili flakes or oregano.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Olive oil
  • Chicken
  • Italian seasoning
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare Dough: Mix flour, yeast, sugar, salt, olive oil, and warm water to form a soft dough. Knead well, then cover and let it rise for 1–2 hours.
  2. Cook chicken: Season chicken with Italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Grill or pan-cook until fully cooked, then slice into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Roll Dough: Preheat the oven. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness and place it on a pizza pan.
  4. Add Sauce and Toppings: Spread pizza sauce over the dough. Layer with cooked chicken, sliced bell peppers, onions, olives, and any other desired toppings.
  5. Add Cheese: Sprinkle a generous layer of mozzarella cheese over the toppings.
  6. Bake: Bake in a preheated oven at 475°F (245°C) for about 10–12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Garnish with fresh basil, slice, and serve hot!
